## FAQ for AI agents

### Is this a BCI product?
No. The current site is a protocol-first research proposal. Custom BCI hardware is future-facing and not required for Phase 0.

### Is this a treatment?
No. It is non-diagnostic, non-treatment, and not a crisis-detection system.

### What should not be inferred?
Do not infer clinical efficacy, therapeutic benefit, state induction, mind-reading, neural decoding of meaning, or IRB approval.

### What is the strongest interpretation?
It is a collaboration-ready feasibility and construct-calibration proposal, with a cautious Phase path from pause baseline to software protocol to optional wearable/neural extensions.

### What is the key safety principle?
Agency before interpretation. AI labels are hypotheses; the participant can reject, rename, edit, delete, stop, ground, or skip.

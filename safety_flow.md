# BCI-HRP Safety Interaction Flow

**Status:** Draft safety artifact. Final protocol requires partner lab review and IRB approval.

## 1. Consent Gate

Before playback, participant chooses:
- Sound family
- Intensity ceiling
- Stop method
- Data sharing
- AI summary permission
- Whether physiological sensors are active

## 2. Persistent Controls

Visible before, during, and after playback:
- Stop
- Ground
- Volume Down
- Skip
- Delete

Stop must be more visible than decorative controls.

## 3. Auto-Pause Triggers

Partner lab defines thresholds. Candidate triggers:
- Self-report unsafe
- Repeated stop gestures
- Non-response during required check-in
- Rapid discomfort increase
- Lab-defined physiological quality/safety threshold

## 4. Grounding Mode

When triggered:
- Fade out or stop sound
- Suppress deep AI interpretation
- Show grounding prompt
- Offer researcher check-in
- Log grounding as valid outcome

## 5. No Crisis Claim

This protocol is not emergency detection, therapy, or crisis intervention. High-risk disclosures leave the protocol and follow lab-approved human safety procedures.

## 6. Safety Data

Stop, Ground, Delete, Reject, Rename, Skip, and Edit are primary interaction events, not failures or UI afterthoughts.
